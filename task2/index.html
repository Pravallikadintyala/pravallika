    About

    Sri Padmavati Mahila Visvavidyalayam is a state university for women, located in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh, India. Founded in 1983, it is dedicated to empowering women through quality education, promoting research, and offering programs that address the needs of society and industry. The university offers a range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs, with a commitment to fostering knowledge, innovation, and societal development.
